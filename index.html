    How to Play

    • Each player chooses a divisor \( d \).
    • On your turn, click two tokens.
    • The move is legal if (x + y) mod d = 0.
    • If legal, the selected pair is removed.
    • If a player has no legal move, they are eliminated.
    • Elimination continues one-by-one until only one player remains.
    • The last remaining player wins.
    • You may undo or redo moves using the buttons.
